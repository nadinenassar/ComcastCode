# ComcastCode
Exercise: Given a List of Strings replace '&' with "and":
*   * For example:  *   
*   * Tromeo &amp; Juliet  
*   * Platoon  
*   * Kate &amp; Leopold 
*   * Poltergeist III  
*   * Harold and Kumar Go to White Castle  
*   * Clerks II  * 
*   * becomes:  * 
*   * Tromeo and Juliet 
*   * Platoon  
*   * Kate and Leopold  
*   * Poltergeist III  
*   * Harold and Kumar Go to White Castle  
*   * Clerks II  *
